We are looking for enthusiastic and passionate students/academic researchers/industry who would like to join our team and voluntarily contribute to promoting AI. If interested, we would like to hear from you. We need volunteers for the following works:


Event moderator

  • Responsibilities may involve hosting, live-streaming, coordination with experts and participants, etc in our online sessions.

Content/Blog writer

  • Responsibilities may involve curating and typesetting blogs from volunteers.
  • Members are also encouraged to submit blogs on topics in their field of expertise.

Graphic designer

  • Responsibilities may include designing posters, banners, and also graphics for web content or motion graphics for promotion.

UI/UX Developer / API creation

  • Responsibilities may include creating UI and API for web-based applications (such as ML projects) from AAII.

AI challenge organizer

  • AAII is planning to organize ML challenges for students/researchers on real-world databases by collaborating with other organizations.
  • Members would be required to coordinate in organizing AI challenges under the banner of AAII.

AI-related paper reading activities

  • AAII is conducting weekly scientific discussions on a large array of AI/ML topics and their applications. It also involves discussing research papers and other resources by the author or other domain experts.
  • We welcome individuals from academia as well as the industry fraternity to join our intragroup discussions and contribute to the sessions. This will help us to keep updated on the recent developments of AI/ML technologies.
  • Enthusiastic participation in internal activities aimed at learning and improving expertise.
