We are looking for enthusiastic and passionate students/researchers from academia/industry who would like to join our team and voluntarily contribute to promoting AI. If interested we would like to hear from you.


What to expect from AAII?

AAII provides a platform that helps to promote networking with students and professionals working in the field of AI/ML and data science. Members get an opportunity to learn and gain organizational experience of talks, tutorials, workshops, promotions, and reaching a mass target audience. It helps to hone one's skill in AI/ML techniques and research and development. The members also get an opportunity to serve society and work towards the cause of science and technology through the AAII platform.

We need help in the following way -


  1. Event moderation
    • Responsibilities may involve hosting, live-streaming, coordination with experts and participants, etc in our online sessions.
  2. Content creation
    • Responsibilities may involve curating and typesetting AI/ML blogs from volunteers. Members are also encouraged to submit blogs on topics in their field of expertise.
  3. Graphic designing
    • Responsibilities may include designing posters, banners, and also graphics for web content or motion graphics for promotion.
  4. UI Development / API creation
    • Responsibilities may include creating UI and API for web-based applications (such as ML projects) from AAII.
  5. Execute innovative AI projects
    • AAII is currently conducting research projects on AI/ML applications, such as NLP, speech technology, database creation specifically especially for the Assamese language (and for other low-resource languages of Assam). We are eager to welcome students/researchers to join us and volunteer for these projects. Fluency in Python and a basic understanding of NLP techniques are recommended but not mandatory.
    • It requires members to work in close collaboration with experts and professionals on AI projects.
  6. Help in organizing AI challenges
    • AAII is planning to organize ML challenges for students/researchers on real-world databases by collaborating with other organizations.
    • Members would be required to coordinate in organizing AI challenges under the banner of AAII.
  7. Learning activities
    • AAII is conducting weekly scientific discussions on a large array of AI/ML topics and their applications. It also involves discussing research papers and other resources by the author or other domain experts. We welcome individuals from academia as well as the industry fraternity to join our intragroup discussions and contribute to the sessions. This will help us to keep updated on the recent developments of AI/ML technologies.
    • Enthusiastic participation in internal activities aimed at learning and improving expertise.
