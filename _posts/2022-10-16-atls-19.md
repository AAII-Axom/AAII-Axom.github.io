### Speaker's bio
Dr. Cong Zhang is a lecturer in Phonetics and Phonology at Newcastle University (UK). She received her DPhil degree from the University of Oxford. Following her DPhil, she worked as a TTS Linguistics Engineer & Linguist at A-Lab, Rokid Inc., where she worked on various tasks for developing a TTS system, and led a project for developing a Singing TTS.
